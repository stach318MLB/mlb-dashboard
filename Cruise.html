Why Orlando / Port Canaveral is your home base

Cleveland has no cruise port, so you'll fly regardless. Of the family-cruise hubs, Orlando (MCO → Port Canaveral) wins for you: it's the cheapest, most frequent flight from CLE (nonstops on multiple carriers), it's home to the most kid-focused mega-ships, and it pairs naturally with a Disney/Universal add-on day if you ever want one. Miami and Fort Lauderdale are close runners-up and open up MSC's newest ships. I priced around Orlando, noting where a Miami departure changes things.

Option A — Royal Caribbean PREMIUM

5-Night Bahamas & Perfect Day at CocoCay · from Orlando (Port Canaveral)

Representative ship: Oasis/Freedom-class (e.g., Wonder of the Seas or Independence of the Seas)

Sample route: Port Canaveral → Nassau → Perfect Day at CocoCay → Sea day → Port Canaveral

Kids' program: Adventure Ocean, ages 3–12, with a nursery for 6–36 mo

Recommended ages: Excellent for all three of your kids; CocoCay's shallow water park is a hit for 4–8

Casino: Yes — Casino Royale onboard

Cleanliness: Consistently rated high; newer/refurbished ships

Perfect Day at CocoCay, Royal Caribbean's private Bahamian island · Photo: Wikimedia Commons, CC BY-SA

Kid & family highlights:

  • Perfect Day at CocoCay private island (shallow splash areas, beaches)
  • Splashaway Bay toddler/kid aqua park onboard
  • FlowRider surf simulator, rock wall, mini-golf, pools
  • Ice/aqua/Broadway-style shows; arcade

Guest rating: ★★★★½ (≈4.5/5 family reviews)

"Perfect Day at CocoCay alone is worth it — our little ones never wanted to leave the splash zone."

Option B — Carnival BUDGET

5-Day Caribbean · from Miami or Port Canaveral

Representative ship: Carnival Horizon, Vista, or Magic

Sample route: Miami → Grand Turk → Amber Cove (Dominican Rep.) → 2 sea days → Miami

Kids' program: Camp Ocean, ages 2–11; Circle "C" for teens

Recommended ages: Strong for 2–11; Dr. Seuss "Seuss at Sea" is a hit at 4–8

Casino: Yes — large, prominent casino

Cleanliness: Solid; casual, lively atmosphere

Kid & family highlights:

  • WaterWorks slides & splash zones
  • Seuss at Sea parade, story time & green-eggs breakfast
  • SkyRide / ropes course on some ships; mini-golf
  • Family-friendly dining, casual vibe

Guest rating: ★★★★ (≈4.0/5 — best value)

Option C — MSC Cruises VALUE

5-Night Caribbean & Ocean Cay · from Miami or Port Canaveral

Representative ship: MSC Seascape or MSC World America (newest, biggest)

Sample route: Miami → Ocean Cay MSC Marine Reserve → Nassau → sea days → Miami

Kids' program: Doremi Club, ages 3–11, with LEGO & Chicco partnerships for little ones

Recommended ages: Great for 4–8; toddler-friendly facilities stand out

Casino: Yes — onboard casino

Cleanliness: High on the newest ships; modern European styling

Ocean Cay MSC Marine Reserve, MSC's private island · Photo: Wikimedia Commons, CC BY-SA

Kid & family highlights:

  • Kids Sail Free — under-18s sail free (pay taxes/fees) sharing with 2 adults
  • Ocean Cay private island — beaches, lagoon, evening light show
  • Aqua parks, water slides, zip line, F1 simulator
  • LEGO play areas; family pools

Guest rating: ★★★★ (≈4.1/5 — top value)
